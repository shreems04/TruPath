TruPath is a care-tracking platform designed to support individuals who may experience moments of confusion, disorientation, or neurological challenges. The goal of the project is to help caregivers stay connected with their loved ones by providing tools to monitor location, activity, and overall well-being. TruPath combines simple tracking features with a caregiver dashboard so families can quickly understand a person’s current status and respond if something unusual happens. The project was inspired by real situations where families worry about loved ones who may wander away or become unresponsive without warning.
